ME

Colombina is my name, Design is my game, #ff3a1d is my colour and I’m a cat lover.

It’s all about that light bulb moment.

The moment when it all clicks and you think you have the best solution to the problem, that’s what keeps me going. No matter how new and complex an idea could be. It’s what makes me grow as a designer.

I want to challenge myself no matter what. I want my little design heart to start pounding and my design brain to start generating ideas and solutions.

That excitement you get when you have a clean start, to help a client achieve their goals. Everyone has their limits, but it’s out jobs as designers to push that creative eye in those who lack our vision. We need to help them see what potential they have by simply working and collaborating with us.

The journey is never easy but it’s worth it. There will be long nights, sweat and tears but nothing beats a smile on that clients face or better yet on our own.
